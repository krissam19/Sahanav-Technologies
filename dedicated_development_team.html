Dedicated Development Teams

Our dedicated development teams have a proven track record of successfully completing sophisticated Web, software, mobile and custom software products on time and within budget. We excel at developing and managing reliable mission-critical applications and other essential software solutions.

We currently have teams working in various technologies including .Net, J2EE, PHP, SQL Server and Oracle. We have extensive recruiting capability and a database of candidate profiles in various technology and business domains.

We offer the following:

People/Resources

We have access to top talent and consistently build teams of highly technical staff, experienced project managers, architects, programmers, developers, analysts, QA or testers, managerial staff and other technical professionals. We work with our clients by facilitating the hiring process to find, evaluate and recruit the team. The team will then be working exclusively for your company making use of Sahanav’s infrastructure under your close direction. We know that people are our most valuable resources. With that in mind, we manage the career development goals of all our employees to ensure job satisfaction and low turnover. This means that customer’s projects are staffed appropriately and remain stable.

Communication

We understand that communication is critical in managing outsourced teams. In order to facilitate communication we provide various tools such as instant messaging, voice over IP, internet email and Skype and will also work with your IT staff to augment these tools as necessary.

Infrastructure

Sahanav’s infrastructure includes network, servers and workstations. Different development platforms are available and configurable based on client needs. Redundant internet connections ensure 24 hour connectivity and we monitor our internet usage to ensure all our teams have the necessary bandwidth.

Processes

Although we have our own methodology and processes, we believe the dedicated team is an extension of your internal team. We will work with you to ensure a seamless plan for development, communication and deliverables.

Strong project management techniques.

Sahanav’s project managers are an experienced group of professionals, possessing excellent communication skills. We will provide you with dedicated or shared project management services based on your needs.
